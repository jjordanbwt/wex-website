01
FinOps Foundation

Build the Foundation for Cloud Financial Accountability

Most organizations lack the foundational infrastructure to manage cloud costs at scale. Without proper tagging, allocation models, and cross-team processes, cloud spend is opaque to the people making the decisions that drive it. We build the FinOps foundation your organization needs to understand, allocate, and govern cloud costs effectively.

What We Deliver
Cloud cost tagging strategy and taxonomy design
Chargeback and showback model development
Cost center and business unit allocation frameworks
AWS Cost Explorer and Cost and Usage Report configuration
FinOps maturity assessment and roadmap
Finance and engineering alignment workshops
02
Cost Governance

Governance That Scales With Your Cloud Footprint

As cloud environments grow, governance gaps compound. New teams provision resources without visibility into cost impact, commitments expire without replacement, and forecasting falls behind actual spend patterns. We design governance frameworks that keep cloud costs predictable and accountable at every stage of growth.

What We Deliver
Cloud budget governance and approval workflows
Commitment management (Reserved Instances, Savings Plans) strategy
Anomaly detection and spend alerting
Cloud spend forecasting models
Unit economics and cost-per-transaction measurement
Policy enforcement using AWS Service Control Policies
03
Executive Visibility

Cloud Cost Reporting Built for Leadership

Finance leaders and executives need cloud cost visibility that translates infrastructure spend into business terms — not raw AWS billing data. We design executive-ready cost reporting that surfaces trends, explains variances, ties cloud investment to business outcomes, and gives leadership the information they need to make informed decisions.

What We Deliver
Executive cloud cost dashboard design and deployment
Monthly cloud P&L reporting by business unit
Board-ready cloud investment summaries
Cost trend analysis and variance explanations
Cloud ROI measurement frameworks
Quarterly FinOps review and optimization planning sessions
