Cost Discovery

Find What You Are Actually Paying For

The first step in cloud cost optimization is honest visibility. We connect directly to your AWS environment and analyze your actual infrastructure in real time. The findings and recommendations are specific to your current setup, not generic benchmarks drawn from someone else's workload.

Common Findings
Right-sizing opportunities for underutilized EC2 and RDS instances
Reserved Instance and Savings Plans coverage gaps
Idle or orphaned resources (unattached EBS, unused Elastic IPs, stale snapshots)
Data transfer inefficiencies and cross-region cost drivers
Storage tier optimization across S3 and EBS
Serverless architecture opportunities to replace over-provisioned infrastructure
