Global Service Delivery

Working Excellence provides flexible team structures so organizations can scale talent, control cost, and maintain operational quality. Depending on the needs of the engagement, we offer several delivery models.

Full Dedicated USA Based Teams

Fully dedicated teams based in the United States that operate as an extension of your organization, aligned to your culture, communication style, and time zones. Ideal for close collaboration, regulatory alignment, and consistent real-time engagement.

Nearshore Teams

Highly skilled professionals located in nearby regions operating in similar time zones to the United States. This model balances cost efficiency with strong collaboration and real-time communication.

Offshore Teams

Access a deep global talent pool while significantly improving cost efficiency. These teams are structured, trained, and managed to deliver consistent quality and productivity while supporting round-the-clock operations.

Blended Team Model

Combines United States based leadership with nearshore or offshore specialists. This approach creates the best balance of quality, responsiveness, scalability, and cost control. We manage the structure so the team operates as one unified delivery group.

Working Excellence builds the right team structure around the work. Whether you need a fully domestic team or a blended global model, the focus stays the same: strong leadership, accountable delivery, and measurable results.
